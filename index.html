Blog

    My Travels


    My hobby is to travel. Here are some images from the places I visited in cities across Albania and neighboring countries. Traveling allows me to explore the beauty of nature, learn about new cultures, and create unforgettable memories. One of the most beautiful cities I have visited is Saranda, a precious part of Albania known for its crystal-clear waters, stunning beaches, and the magical Butrint National Park. Saranda's sunsets are truly a sight to behold, making it a top destination for anyone visiting Albania. In Kosovo, I was amazed by the charm of Prizren, a city with cobblestone streets, Ottoman-era architecture, and the iconic Stone Bridge. The city offers a mix of historical landmarks and vibrant culture, with delicious food and welcoming people. Exploring North Macedonia, I fell in love with Lake Ohrid, one of the oldest and deepest lakes in Europe. The town of Ohrid is a UNESCO World Heritage site, known for its ancient churches, monasteries, and breathtaking lake views. Visiting neighboring countries has also shown me the unique beauty and diversity of the Balkan region. Each destination has its own story, culture, and natural wonders to discover. From the Albanian Alps to the vibrant streets of Skopje and the serene landscapes of Kosovo, every trip has been an adventure worth remembering.
